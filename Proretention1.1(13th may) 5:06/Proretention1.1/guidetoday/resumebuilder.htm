Create online your resume


You may directly upload your resume or use any of the two options below:


1. Text Editor

When using this option you have a rich text editor at your disposal - you can write and format the text of your resume or directly paste it as rich text. The employers will be able to search by keyword in the text of your resume. Using this option is faster but it gives you worse chances that your resume will be found when the employers make searches. 		  2. Resume Creator

When using the resume creator you will be asked to fill the information for your work history, education etc. in multiple fields. Even that it could take a bit more time to create your cv using this option, we recommend it because the employers have better chance to find your resume - all the different fields in your resume will be searchable.
